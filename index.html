About Us

At the AYBU E-sports Club, our primary goal is to introduce the world of e-sports to the wider student community at our school and help them achieve success in this exciting and rapidly growing field. We believe that e-sports is much more than just a hobby or pastime - it's a legitimate profession that requires dedication, skill, and a deep understanding of gaming culture and technology. By providing our members with access to a wide range of resources and facilities, as well as opportunities to participate in events and competitions, we hope to foster a love of gaming and e-sports in the next generation of gamers. Ultimately, we believe that by promoting the values of sportsmanship, teamwork, and fair play within the context of e-sports, we can help our members achieve success both on and off the virtual battlefield. For more detailed information, you can go to the About link.

Our Events

The AYBU E-sports Club is committed to providing our members with a wide range of engaging and exciting events throughout the year. From e-sports tournaments featuring popular games like Valorant, League of Legends, and Overwatch, to meetups and group outings that allow our members to connect with each other and explore new gaming experiences, we strive to offer something for everyone. Additionally, we frequently host workshops, seminars, and guest lectures featuring experts from the gaming industry, as well as opportunities to participate in national and international e-sports competitions. By participating in these events, our members not only have the chance to showcase their skills and compete at the highest levels, but also to make meaningful connections with other passionate gamers and industry professionals.
