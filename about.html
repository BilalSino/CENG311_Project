About AYBU E-sports Club

AYBU E-sports Club is a student organization at Ankara Yildirim Beyazit University (AYBU) that focuses on competitive gaming and e-sports. Our club is open to all AYBU students who are passionate about gaming and want to participate in tournaments, meet new people, and improve their skills.

We organize regular events and tournaments both online and offline, and participate in national and international competitions. Our main focus is on games like League of Legends, Dota 2, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Overwatch, and Fortnite, but we also welcome players of other games.

Our club members have access to a variety of resources and facilities, including gaming computers, gaming consoles, VR equipment, and streaming equipment. We also have a dedicated gaming room on campus where members can hang out, practice, and play together.

If you want to join our club, please fill in the member registration form.
